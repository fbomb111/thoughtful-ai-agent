# Foxen - Customer Support Knowledge Base

## About Foxen

Foxen is a property insurance company that helps property managers, lenders, and HOAs track insurance coverage, monitor compliance, and manage risk across property portfolios.

## Insurance Tracking

Foxen centralizes policy data across properties, tracking coverage status, renewals, and expirations to ensure portfolios stay continuously insured.

The platform surfaces upcoming expirations, coverage gaps, and missing documentation so teams can resolve issues before they become compliance or risk problems.

## Compliance Monitoring

Foxen monitors required insurance documents and endorsements to validate compliance against lender, HOA, or property-specific requirements.

Compliance checks flag missing or outdated certificates, insufficient coverage limits, or policy exclusions so teams can take corrective action quickly.

## Risk Management

Foxen provides portfolio-level visibility into risk exposure, helping stakeholders prioritize remediation and reduce the likelihood of uninsured losses.

Risk insights can be used to identify high-risk properties, recurring compliance gaps, and patterns that require proactive attention.

## Benefits of Using Foxen

Using Foxen can reduce administrative overhead, improve compliance accuracy, and help teams resolve issues faster across large property portfolios.

Key benefits include:
- **Reduced administrative burden**: Centralized tracking minimizes manual follow-ups and spreadsheet work.
- **Improved compliance**: Automated checks catch missing documents and coverage gaps early.
- **Faster issue resolution**: Proactive alerts keep renewals and corrections on schedule.
- **Lower risk exposure**: Portfolio-level visibility helps prevent uninsured losses.
- **Scalability**: Manage large and growing property portfolios without proportional staffing increases.
